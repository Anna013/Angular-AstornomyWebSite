Observations


Observations near the Petrovaradin Fortress every three days-see you!
Mon, Wen, Sat at 21:00h

The Astronomical Society of Novi Sad (ADNOS) is the oldest astronomical society in Vojvodina. It was founded in 1974, and today has about 300 members. The Novi Sad Astronomical Observatory at the Petrovaradin Fortress is a facility that enables the realization of observation activities and processing of the obtained data and photographs. Observation always starts at 21:00h, adress: Petrovaradin Fortres 11. Hope we see you!
